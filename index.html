01 — ABOUT

About me

My name is Pranesh Rai, originally from Bhojpur in eastern Nepal and currently based in Kathmandu. I am a passionate Cinematographer, Video Editor, Documentary Filmmaker, Photographer, and Drone/FPV Pilot with over 3 years of professional experience in visual storytelling.

I enjoy capturing authentic moments and transforming them into compelling visual narratives that connect with audiences. For me, the camera is more than just a tool — it is a medium for creativity, expression, and storytelling. Through documentaries, music videos, promotional content, and cinematic productions, I strive to create meaningful and visually engaging content that leaves a lasting impact.

I am deeply interested in exploring different perspectives, emotions, cultures, and stories through filmmaking. With a strong commitment to quality, creativity, and continuous learning, I constantly refine my skills to deliver professional and impactful visual experiences.
